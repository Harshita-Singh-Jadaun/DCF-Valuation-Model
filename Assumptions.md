## Assumptions Used
- Forecast period: 5 years
- Revenue growth: Assumption-based (scenario-driven)
- Operating margins: Historical trend + stability assumption
- Tax rate: As per effective tax range
- CAPEX & Working Capital: Linked to revenue assumptions
- Discount rate (WACC): Based on cost of equity and capital structure
- Terminal growth rate: Conservative long-term growth assumption
